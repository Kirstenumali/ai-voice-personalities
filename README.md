# ChatGPT Voices

This repository documents a set of voice personalities designed for conversational AI systems. Each voice has a unique tone, speaking style, and personality to create different interaction experiences.

## Available Voices

### Ash

**Style:** Fully American male voice
**Description:**
Ash speaks with a clear, confident American accent. His tone is natural, calm, and direct, making him suitable for general conversations, assistants, and informational responses.

### Fable

**Style:** American male voice with Gen Z tone
**Description:**
Fable has a modern, relaxed Gen Z speaking style. His voice feels casual, energetic, and conversational, making interactions feel more friendly and relatable.

### Shimer

**Style:** Expressive and vibrant voice
**Description:**
Shimer has a lively and expressive tone. The voice feels playful, dramatic, and colorful, making it ideal for entertaining interactions and engaging conversations.

### Sage

**Style:** Theater-style female voice
**Description:**
Sage speaks like a stage performer. Her voice is expressive, dramatic, and articulate, giving responses a theatrical and storytelling quality.

### Coral

**Style:** Clear and articulate speaker
**Description:**
Coral focuses on delivering sentences clearly and smoothly. This voice works well for narration, explanations, and any situation where clarity is important.

## Purpose

These voice profiles are designed to:

* Create more **human-like AI conversations**
* Allow developers to **choose personalities for assistants**
* Improve **engagement and user experience**

## Usage

These voices can be used in:

* AI assistants
* Robots and interactive systems
* Voice interfaces
* Educational tools
* Entertainment applications
